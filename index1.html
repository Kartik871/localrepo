In the quiet hours after midnight, when the world seems to hold its breath, there exists a secret garden where time moves differently. The moonlight filters through ancient oak trees, casting silver shadows that dance across cobblestone paths worn smooth by countless footsteps. Here, fireflies weave intricate patterns in the air, their gentle glow illuminating flowers that bloom only in darkness. The air carries the scent of night-blooming jasmine and the distant sound of a fountain whose waters have flowed for over a century. This mystical place serves as a sanctuary for dreamers and wanderers, offering solace to those who seek beauty in the quiet moments between one day and the next.
